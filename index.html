Digital Capital | 数字资本

introduction

Digital Capital was founded by Felix, a graduate of the 2017 Chemistry Department of Renmin University of China. It is a professional investment institution focusing on the blockchain field, focusing on establishing an ecological layout in mining pools, trading platforms, technology media, and technology applications, and is committed to Investing and incubating outstanding entrepreneurial teams in the field of blockchain to promote the innovation and development of blockchain technology. Digital Capital is a non-profit foundation registered under the approval of the Accounting and Corporate Regulatory Authority of Singapore (ACRA), under the category of Public company limited by guarantee.

简介

数字资本由中国人民大学2017届化学系本科毕业校友Felix创办，是一家聚焦于区块链领域的专业投资机构，专注在矿池、交易平台、科技媒体、技术应用等方向上建立生态布局，致力于投资和孵化区块链领域的优秀创业团队，推动区块链技术革新与发展。数字资本是在新加坡会计与企业发展局（ACRA）的批准下，注册成立的非盈利性基金会，类别为 Publiccompanylimitedbyguarantee。
